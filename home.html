Asiasbook is a website designed to help you highlight waste management issues in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Users can upload images and location details wherever they find this problem after they have registered.

What is our goal at Asiasbook?

Our goal is to create a simple and effective platform for our users to help bring raise awareness about this ongoing issue.
